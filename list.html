Product Requirements — School Finance & Operations

This document outlines the pages and components to be developed for the International School finance and operations system. Each card links to the expected page or module.

Core Financial Pages

Financial Summary Report

One-page A4, Apple-style financial report including Income, Expenses, Assets (CapEx), Investments, subtotals, and student counts.

High Priority HTML • Print-ready

Detailed Profit & Loss

Expandable, multi-period P&L with drill-down by category, exportable to PDF and Excel.

High Priority Web • Data-driven

Assets & Capital Expenditure

Fixed assets register, construction costs, equipment, depreciation readiness, and summaries by year.

Medium Priority Web • Tables

Investments & Funding

Investment inflows by partner, period comparisons, totals, and reconciliation against cash usage.

Medium Priority Web • Finance

Analytics & Insights

Student Metrics Dashboard

Student counts by academic year, intake vs growth, revenue per student, and trends.

Medium Priority Dashboard

Executive KPIs

Key metrics such as margins, cost ratios, YoY growth, and financial health indicators.

Medium Priority Charts • Summary

High Priority Print • Compliance
